About

Roman Prikryl is a Zurich-based multimedia artist and digital consultant. With a background in new media, sound, and Web3 tools, his work spans visual design, immersive audio, and experimental mindfulness formats. From his practice space on Zypressenstrasse, he offers creative consulting, digital production, and facilitation of reflective events and sound-based experiences under the label atelierrp.

Current projects

Hardcore Yoga Nidra

HCYN (Hardcore Yoga Nidra) is a minimal, immersive sound-based practice exploring the liminal space between wakefulness and sleep. Lying down, participants are guided through stillness, body awareness, and abstract imagery — using subtle textures, low-frequency sound, and spacious timing to deepen attention and dissolve boundaries.

It's not for "relaxation" in the traditional sense — but an invitation to rest inside the fabric of perception itself.
